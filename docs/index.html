What WIN Does

We're the link between a wildlife emergency in the field and the rehabber who can treat it.

Wildlife In Need PA is a volunteer-run dispatch and rescue network. When the public calls our hotline to report a sick, injured, or orphaned wild animal, our responders handle safe capture and transport to a licensed rehabilitation partner.

Every responder is a volunteer. Every dollar donated goes directly to fuel, traps, carriers, and the supplies that keep rescues moving. We're a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit.

⚠ Animals WIN cannot capture

  • Deer over 30 lbs
  • Bears
  • Adult otters, fishers, bobcats
  • Venomous snakes

For any of these, please contact the Pennsylvania Game Commission or a specialized service.
